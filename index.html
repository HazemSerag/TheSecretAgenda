Where it all started

The first time we met, was in Starbucks New-York 03/08/2018. It was an awesome day in general and special day because i found YOU !

It was the first day to meet alone 27/09/2018, after long chats, likes and stalking each other. This day was weird but was lovely. It was the sparkle of our journey.

I call it 'The Green Day' 05/10/2018. It was the day that we fell in love together, it was the day how we treat each other like lovers, it was the day that i realized that you are the one and it was the day that i choose to say 'I Love You'.

Officially we are finaly together, i remember all the details of this day. The lovely plants, the food on the beach, our coffee and central park. I love your company !.
